About us

At Orbitz, we are passionate about crafting a unique and delightful beverage experience for our customers. Founded with a vision for innovation in the beverage industry, we have been serving the world with our exceptional fruit bubble drink since the early 1990s. Our dedication to creativity and quality has made us a name synonymous with excitement and wonder.

Our Fruit Bubble Drink

Introducing our star product - the Orbitz Fruit Bubble Drink! This extraordinary drink is a fusion of all-natural fruit juices from exotic and tropical fruits, carefully blended to perfection. What makes it truly magical is the captivating display of floating, edible gelatinous spheres within the beverage. With every sip, these spheres burst with flavorful delight, creating a visual spectacle that will leave you amazed.

Mesmerizing Visuals, Unforgettable Taste

The visual magic of our floating spheres is a treat for the eyes, but it’s the taste that truly captivates your palate. Each sip of Orbitz Fruit Bubble Drink is a burst of refreshment and sweetness, appealing to all ages. It’s a drink that will elevate any occasion, from parties to casual get-togethers, and bring smiles to the faces of everyone who experiences it.

Quality is Our Priority

We take pride in the quality of our Fruit Bubble Drink. Our team of experts ensures that only the finest and freshest fruits make their way into every bottle. With rigorous quality control measures, we guarantee a consistently delightful experience that meets your highest expectations.
